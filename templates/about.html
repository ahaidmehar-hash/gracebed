Our Philosophy

Three Rules We Never Break

🔩
Rule 1

No screws on visible surfaces. Every joint that the eye can find must be a wood joint — mortise, tenon, or dovetail.

🌳
Rule 2

Only solid, sustainably sourced hardwoods. Oak, walnut, cherry, and ash — nothing engineered, nothing hollow.

✦
Rule 3

Every bed must pass a 4-hour inspection by our founder before delivery. No exceptions. No shortcuts. No compromises.
